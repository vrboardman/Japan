Meiji Jingu ShrineSantuario Meiji Jingu
Free — main grounds¥500 — inner garden optional
Built in 1920 to enshrine Emperor Meiji and Empress Shoken, this shrine sits inside a 70-hectare forested sanctuary — entirely artificial, planted by 100,000 volunteers using 365 tree species donated from every Japanese prefecture. After 100+ years it feels like ancient old-growth forest. The 700-meter wooded sandori approach creates a profound transition from Harajuku's commercial bustle to sacred calm. Look for the sake barrel wall (kazaridaru) and the wooden ema wishing plaque wall. Después de más de 100 años parece bosque antiguo primigenio. El camino sandori arbolado de 700 metros crea una transición profunda del bullicio comercial de Harajuku a la calma sagrada. At 599m, Takao-san is Japan's most-visited mountain. Trail 1 (paved, steeper) passes the Yakuo-in Shingon Buddhist temple complex with dramatic tengu demon statues and cedar trees — best for culture. Trail 6 (unpaved, stream-side) crosses the Biwa waterfall, follows a mountain stream, and is less crowded — recommended for a genuine hike feel. Both reach the same summit. Late May means vivid fresh green canopy, cool temperatures, and clear air. Summit views: Mt. Fuji to the west, entire Tokyo skyline to the east.Con 599m, el Takao-san es la montaña más visitada de Japón. El Sendero 6 (sin pavimentar, junto al arroyo) cruza la cascada Biwa, sigue un arroyo de montaña y tiene menos gente — recomendado para una experiencia de senderismo auténtica. Ambos alcanzan la misma cima. Finales de mayo significa un dosel verde fresco y vívido, temperaturas frescas y aire claro. Cast in 1252, this 13.35-meter bronze Amida Buddha once sat inside a giant wooden hall — the hall was destroyed by 14th–15th century typhoons and tsunamis, leaving the Buddha to sit permanently open-air. The weathered green patina and sheer scale (93 tons of bronze) are deeply impressive. You can enter the hollow interior and look up through the Buddha's shoulders — a strange, meditative experience. La pátina verde envejecida y la escala colosal (93 toneladas de bronce) son profundamente impresionantes.
